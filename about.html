My name is Annie. School puts a heavy emphasis on sport as an integral part of education. I have always enjoyed sports and I have a passion for fitness, martial arts, dance, jogging and a holistic approach to health. I have been a Personal Trainer and Fitness Instructor (since 2009) but started my sports journey many years earlier. I keep educating myself in both fitness and plant-based diet areas. I run fitness classes in Killarney, where I do my best to combine different styles such as: Traditional exercises to martial art techniques or even dance! to create the ultimate, unique, and effective workouts.

It became my passion and I want to share it with YOU!

As a mother of two, I know exactly how our body changes during and after pregnancy. I specialise in women fitness classes and Personal Training, but don’t be misled by the colour pink, expect­ a real workout! I believe in a holistic approach, trust in nature and common sense. It’s not only about dieting and exercising, but lifestyle, consciousness and wellbeing!
